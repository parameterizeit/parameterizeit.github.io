    Summer lab research assistant

    2023-05-25

    Open to continuing NCSU students

    Download this ad (pdf)

    The Diversity in Changing Environments Lab seeks to hire a part-time (~10 hours/week) NCSU student worker to support our research on microbial community ecology during summer 2023.

    Weekly schedule: Part-time, June-August 2023

    Exact work days and times are negotiable, but ideally splits the hours over 2-3 days M-F between 8am-5pm and is consistent week-to-week. There is potential to continue part-time work during the academic year.

    Pay: $15-18 / hour

    Depending on experience.

    Job duties:

    The successful candidate will support our ongoing lab experiments to study coexistence of microbes. The primary job duties will focus on logistical support tasks including:

    • Washing and sterilizing glassware used during experiments
    • Preparing media for culturing microbes
    • Receiving and organizing supply shipments
    • Maintaining a clean and safe lab environment

    Additional job duties for those interested in engaging more with our research may include:

    • Feeding and transferring microbes to new media
    • Collecting experimental data on microbes under a microscope
    • Assisting with experiment set-up and maintenance tasks

    Required experience:

    Open to continuing NCSU students who have completed at least 1 biology lab class or have previously worked in a research lab. Candidates who have completed a microbiology lab course will be preferred.

    To apply:

    Please email Dr. Will Petry ( wpetry@gmail.com) with the following information & “Lab assistant application” as the email subject line. Review begins 2 June & continues until position is filled.

    1. A brief description of how your class and/or prior work experience prepares you for lab work
    2. A brief description of what you hope to gain from a research lab work experience
    3. Your weekly work availability & preferred schedule (between Monday-Friday 8am-5pm)
    4. Your ideal start date
